Travel Information

Address

The conference takes place at Native Space, Schlesische Straße 30, 10997 Berlin.

Public Transport

The public transport stops closest to Native Space are

  • Bus stop Taborstraße (2min walk)
  • U Schlesisches Tor (7min walk)
  • S Warschauer Straße (12min walk)

If you have an Android Phone, the Transportr app works very well with the public transport API of Berlin.

Airplane

Berlin’s two major airports Schönefeld (SXF) and Tegel (TXL) are fairly well connected with public transport. However, Schönefeld airport might be faster to get to/ travel from.

Train

If you’re coming in by train (with Deutsche Bahn), you can either go to Berlin Hauptbahnhof (Main Station), or Berlin Ostbahnhof (East Station).

While the latter is closer to the location, both are connect to public transport by S-Bahn (local train) and bus.
